OSIRiS

Officeworks Store Initialization Routine and Scripts

Life, Death, Afterlife and Rebirth. Osiris is a well known ancient Egyptian god

characterised as being a green faced Pharaoh with mummy-wrapped legs and

an ornate crown. He also carries a characteristic Egyptian crook (See Logo)
and flail.


OSIRiS is capable of moulding a display/demo computer to it's own image and
subsequently restoring it later. The connotations of Life, Death and rebirth should

be fairly apparent.
